OUR STORY

Fashion for every family,
at prices that feel lucky.

We started Lucky Kidies Wears with one simple belief — every child deserves to look good, and every parent deserves to afford it.

How it started

Born from a mother's frustration

Like many Nigerian parents, our founder knew the struggle of dressing growing children without breaking the bank. Kids outgrow clothes in weeks, not months — yet new clothes never get cheaper. Lucky Kidies Wears was born from that frustration, with a mission to make quality fashion affordable for every family.

Family First

Every decision we make starts with one question: would we buy this for our own family?

What we do

Thrift & new, under one roof

We carefully source and curate both brand new and pre-loved clothing for children, women, and men. Every thrift item is inspected for quality before it reaches you. Every new item is selected for value. The result? A shop where you never have to compromise between quality and price.

Sustainable Fashion

Giving good clothes a second life isn't just affordable — it's the right thing to do for our planet.

Our promise

You shop with confidence

We know trust is earned. That's why we describe every item honestly, provide clear photos, offer easy returns, and make ourselves available on WhatsApp before, during, and after your order. When you shop with Lucky Kidies, you're not just buying clothes — you're joining a community.

Our Guarantee

If something isn't right with your order, we'll make it right. No stress, no runaround.
